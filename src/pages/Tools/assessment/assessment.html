Operational Stress Injury vs. Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder


An operational stress injury is any persistent psychological difficulty you are experiencing because of your work duties. It is the term used to describe a broad range of problems which includes post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) and any other diagnosed conditions, such as anxiety disorders, depression, and other conditions that could interfere with your daily functioning.
