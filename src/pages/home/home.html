In corrections, we see all kinds of difficult situations so it's important to take care of our mental health just as you do our physical health.
The state of our mental health is shown through how we think, feel and act in response to the challenges of daily life.

Originating from an organization wide Hackathon held Oct 2016 it was decided that CSC as a whole would come together to show support and assist our colleagues with improved access to programs set up to help us prepare and recover from stressful situations and get back to work smoothly.
